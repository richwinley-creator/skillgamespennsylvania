FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Are skill games legal in Pennsylvania?

Yes - and the case is now before the PA Supreme Court. In December 2023, Pennsylvania's Commonwealth Court unanimously ruled that skill-based games are legal games of skill. The Supreme Court heard oral arguments on November 20, 2025, and a decision is pending. While the case is under review, skill games remain legal and operational throughout Pennsylvania, with approximately 80,000 machines in operation statewide.

How much can I earn?

Typical earnings range from $1,500-$5,000+ per machine monthly. Bars and game rooms tend to be highest performers ($3,000-6,000/machine), while convenience stores typically see $1,500-2,500/machine. Your share depends on whether you're on revenue share (35-50%) or own the machines outright (100%).

What's the difference between leasing and buying?

Leasing has no upfront cost - we provide the machines and split revenue with you. Buying means you own the machines outright and keep all profits, but have an upfront investment. Both options include our support.

How long does installation take?

Most locations go from first call to earning within two weeks. Once you're approved, we typically deliver and install within 10 business days. Installation itself takes just a few hours - we handle everything from delivery to setup and training.

What if a machine breaks down?

We provide full maintenance and repair services. For leased machines, this is included at no extra cost. For purchased machines, we offer affordable service plans with rapid response times.

Which brand should I choose?

It depends on your location and customer base. Banilla is great for high-traffic bars, JVL offers the widest game selection with progressive jackpots, and Primero is perfect for new operators. We'll help you choose.
