How Much Can You Earn with Skill Games in Pennsylvania?

Updated January 2025 | 7 min read

One of the first questions business owners ask is "How much money can I actually make with skill games?" Here are real numbers based on Pennsylvania locations across different business types.

Factors That Affect Your Revenue

1. Foot Traffic

The single biggest factor in skill game revenue is how many people visit your location. High-traffic locations in busy areas consistently outperform quieter spots.

2. Operating Hours

Locations open late (especially bars) tend to earn more. The evening and late-night hours are peak playing times. A bar open until 2 AM will typically earn more than one that closes at midnight.

3. Demographics

Blue-collar areas and working-class neighborhoods often show stronger skill game performance. Players tend to be adults 25-55 with disposable income for entertainment.

4. Machine Placement

Visibility matters. Machines placed in high-traffic areas of your business (near the bar, by the entrance) outperform those tucked in back corners.

5. Number of Machines

More machines generally means more revenue, but there's a sweet spot based on your location size. We help determine the optimal number for your space.

6. Machine Quality and Variety

Premium machines with popular games attract more players. Having a mix of different cabinets keeps players engaged.

Real Examples from Pennsylvania

Philadelphia Neighborhood Bar

A corner bar in South Philadelphia with 3 machines earns approximately $3,200/month for the owner. Open until 2 AM with steady local traffic.

Pittsburgh Convenience Store

A busy c-store near a residential area with 2 machines brings in about $1,800/month. The machines draw customers who also make purchases.

Allentown Game Room

A dedicated game room with 12 machines generates over $12,000/month for the owner. Located on a busy commercial strip with ample parking.

How to Maximize Your Earnings

  1. Optimal placement: Put machines where customers naturally gather and can see them
  2. Keep them running: Report any issues immediately for quick service
  3. Promote awareness: Let customers know the games are there
  4. Maintain your location: Clean, well-lit areas perform better
  5. Right number of machines: Work with us to determine the ideal count
