Are Skill Games Legal in Harrisburg?

Yes. Skill games are legal in Harrisburg and throughout Pennsylvania. In December 2023, Pennsylvania's Commonwealth Court unanimously ruled that skill-based games are legal games of skill. The case is now before the Pennsylvania Supreme Court, which heard oral arguments on November 20, 2025. A decision is pending.

The key distinction: skill games require player skill to determine outcomes, unlike chance-based gambling. A skilled player can win consistently through pattern recognition and decision-making. While the Supreme Court reviews the case, skill games remain legal and operational statewide, with approximately 80,000 machines currently in operation across Pennsylvania.
